Lemon

Lemons are sour superfruits that are native to South Asia. Their luminous yellow color and zingy taste makes them beloved to many. Lemons have a very low pH, and are rich in Vitamin C! Hooray!

Try...

crafting delicious lemon bars! Create a shortbread crust with flour and butter. Whip together a filling of sugar, eggs, and lemon juice. Bake, let cool, and enjoy!
